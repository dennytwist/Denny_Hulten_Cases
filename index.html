About

The work
behind
the work.

Creative direction is not decoration. It is the difference between an event people attend and one they remember. And between a client you win once and a client who stays.

I work at the intersection of strategy and production: building the narrative first, then the experience around it.

Background

Swedish creative director with over 20 years in live event production. Started with Gothenburg Dance Festival, one of Scandinavia's largest urban expression competitions, with 10,000+ participants across eight consecutive years. Expanded into international corporate events, live television, and national cultural programmes funded by the Swedish government.

Approach

Every project starts with a single question: what is the real problem? Not the brief as stated, but the underlying need. The concept follows from that answer. When I reframe the problem correctly, the solution tends to be both simpler and more effective. Clients come back because of it.

Currently

Delivering "Early Access", a three-year international culture programme for Play'n GO across seven countries (Sweden, Spain, UK, Malta, Hungary, Poland, Philippines). Serving as creative director, concept architect, and on-stage host for all events. Based in Gothenburg, available for strategic creative and production briefs internationally.
